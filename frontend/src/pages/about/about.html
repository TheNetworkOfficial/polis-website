About Polis

Built for local civic participation at neighborhood scale

Polis is a civic/social mobile app for iOS and Android designed to help people discover what is happening in their local area and participate in meaningful community discussions.

Mission

Make local information easier to discover and discuss with tools that support safety, accountability, and user choice.

Why local civic social

Most conversations about policy, services, and neighborhood issues are geographically relevant. Polis prioritizes local context to reduce noise and improve usefulness.

Service architecture and providers

Polis uses infrastructure and service providers that support authentication, hosting, media workflows, and location-related utilities.

  • AWS services including Cognito, DynamoDB, S3, Lambda/API Gateway, and CloudWatch
  • Cloudflare Images/Stream for media processing and delivery
  • Google Places API for event address autocomplete in app flows
  • AWS Location and U.S. Census geocoder support in address validation workflows
