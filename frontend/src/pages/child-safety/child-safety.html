Child Safety Standards (CSAE/CSAM) - Published Standards

App: Polis

Developer/Operator: Lux Corp

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Polis is committed to maintaining a safe environment and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation.

1) Our standards against CSAE

Polis has zero tolerance for content or behavior that exploits, endangers, sexualizes, grooms, or abuses minors.

  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming or enticement of minors
  • Sextortion involving minors
  • Trafficking-related exploitation
  • Solicitation of sexual content involving minors

2) CSAM policy and appropriate action

CSAM is illegal. When Polis obtains actual knowledge of CSAM, actions may include:

  • Removing or disabling access to content
  • Restricting content visibility
  • Suspending or terminating involved accounts
  • Preserving relevant information where legally required
  • Reporting to appropriate authorities where required or appropriate

3) In-app reporting mechanism (user feedback)

Polis provides in-app tools for reporting policy violations and safety concerns.

  • Users can report content such as posts or event content.
  • Users can block or mute other users.
  • Safety support contact: lux.corp.app@gmail.com

Emergency note: If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services.

4) Child protection and access by minors

Polis is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly allow or maintain underage accounts.

5) Compliance with child safety laws

Polis aims to comply with applicable child safety laws and regulations, including obligations related to CSAE/CSAM handling and reporting.
